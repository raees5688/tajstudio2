Return Policy

At Taj Studio, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you are not satisfied, we offer a straightforward return process. Please review our policy below for details on eligibility, timelines, and procedures.

Important: All returns must be initiated within 14 days of receiving your order. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original condition with tags attached.

Eligibility for Returns

  • Items must be returned within 14 days of delivery
  • Products must be in original condition with all tags attached
  • Proof of purchase (order number) is required
  • Final sale items are not eligible for return (marked as such on product page)
  • Personalized or custom-made items cannot be returned

How to Initiate a Return

  1. Log in to your Taj Studio account
  2. Navigate to "Order History" and select the order containing items you wish to return
  3. Select the items for return and choose a reason
  4. Print the prepaid return shipping label that will be generated
  5. Package your items securely and attach the label
  6. Drop off the package at any designated shipping location

Return Processing Timeline

Stage Timeframe What Happens
Return Initiation Within 14 days of delivery Start return process via your account
Ship Back Within 5 days of initiation Package items and ship back to us
Processing 3-5 business days after receipt Inspect returned items
Refund 5-7 business days after approval Refund issued to original payment method

Refund Information

Once your return is received and inspected, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. If approved, your refund will be processed and a credit will automatically be applied to your original payment method within 5-7 business days.

Please note that original shipping charges are non-refundable. For returns due to our error (wrong item, defective item, etc.), we will cover return shipping costs.

Return Policy FAQs

Can I exchange an item instead of returning it?

Yes, we offer exchanges for a different size or color of the same item, subject to availability. During the return process, simply select "Exchange" instead of "Return". There are no additional shipping fees for exchanges.

What if I received a damaged or defective item?

Please contact our customer service immediately at support@tajstudio.com with your order number and photos of the damaged item. We will arrange for a free return and send a replacement at no cost to you.

How long does it take to receive my refund?

Refunds are typically processed within 5-7 business days after we receive and approve your return. The time it takes for the refund to appear in your account depends on your payment provider.

Once your order has shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking number and link to track your package. You can also track your order by logging into your Taj Studio account.

Shipping Restrictions

We currently ship to over 100 countries worldwide. Some restrictions apply to certain countries due to customs regulations. If you have any questions about shipping to your location, please contact us before placing your order.

Shipping Policy FAQs

Can I change my shipping address after placing an order?

Address changes can only be made if your order hasn't been processed for shipping yet. Please contact us immediately at support@tajstudio.com with your order number and correct address. We cannot change the address once the order has shipped.

What happens if my package is lost in transit?

If tracking information shows your package as delivered but you haven't received it, please contact us immediately. For packages lost in transit, we will launch an investigation with the carrier and reship your order once confirmed lost.
