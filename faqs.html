How to Measure Yourself

For the best fit, follow these measurement guidelines:

Chest/Bust

Measure around the fullest part of your chest/bust, keeping the tape measure horizontal and snug but not tight.

Waist

Measure around the narrowest part of your natural waist, usually just above the belly button.

Hips

Measure around the fullest part of your hips, about 20cm below your natural waist.

Inseam

Measure from the top of your inner thigh down to the bottom of your ankle.

Size Guide FAQs

What if I'm between sizes?

If your measurements fall between two sizes, we recommend selecting the larger size for a more comfortable fit, especially for tops and outerwear. For tailored items like pants or dresses, you may prefer the smaller size.

Do Taj Studio sizes run true to size?

Our garments are designed to be true to standard sizing. However, some styles may have a relaxed or slim fit. Always check the specific product description for fit notes. If you have any doubts, our customer service team can advise on specific items.

How do I measure my child?

For children, measure their height while standing straight without shoes. For chest and waist, measure while they're wearing light clothing. If your child is between sizes, choose based on their height rather than age, as children grow at different rates.

Can I exchange for a different size?

Yes, we offer free size exchanges within 30 days of purchase. Please see our Return Policy for complete details on how to exchange items for a different size.
